Tutorial

Search TripleE Evaluations Tab
In this tab, you can search for existing TripleE lesson evaluations. You can search based on the following criteria: TripleE lesson evaluation author, TripleE lesson evaluation title, keywords (e.g., technology tools/apps), minimum score, school subjects, or school grade levels.
Evaluate Your Lesson Using TripleE Tab
In this tab, you can submit your own TripleE evaluations for lesson plans. First, you will evaluate your lesson according to the TripleE rubric, giving scores between 0 and 2 for engagement, enhancement, and extension of the learning goals). Next, you will include the following information: TripleE lesson evaluation author, TripleE lesson evaluation title, keywords (e.g., technology tools/apps), minimum score, school subjects, or school grade levels. You can also upload the actual lesson (in PDF).
Account Tab
In this tab, you can login if you aren't already logged in, or logout if you are logged in.
Profile Tab
In this tab, you can view your profile picture, a brief description about yourself, and all your lesson evaluations.
